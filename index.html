P — 01
Camera & Sensor Rig — 4 Iterations to Al 6061
3D print → Wire EDM machined aluminium · Creo Parametric
▼

Inherited a heavy mild-steel mount. Redesigned from scratch — iterating through three 3D-printed prototypes, then finalised in precision wire-EDM machined Aluminium 6061. Final rig mounts multiple perception cameras, Trimble GNSS antenna, and LiDAR with millimeter-level positional accuracy. Each iteration reduced weight and improved field installation speed.

P — 02
Drive-By-Wire System — Tata Nexon
Mechanical brake actuation · AMT gear control · Bowden cable + custom bearing
▼

Tata Nexon has no OEM electronic braking. Engineered a full mechanical brake actuation system using a linear actuator and Bowden cable, with a custom-machined bearing for smooth cable travel. Integrated AMT gear selector with safety switch logic. Also diagnosed and repaired a field fault where the AMT module stopped registering the safety switch required for N→D/R transitions — restored gear-shift functionality under deadline pressure.

Linear ActuatorBowden CableAMTCustom BearingVehicle WiringField Repair
P — 03
Ouster LiDAR & Trimble GNSS Integration
Sensor sync · INS lever arm calibration · PPS timing
▼

Full integration of Ouster OS1-128 LiDAR with Trimble BX992 GNSS/INS system. Configured Ouster interface box for PPS sync and power. Set up Trimble BX992 INS — GNSS lever arm, IMU lever arm, and rear-wheel reference frame in vehicle coordinate system. Critical for accurate positioning during autonomous operation.

Ouster OS1-128Trimble BX992RTK/PPKPPS SyncIMU Fusion
P — 04
Field Fault Diagnosis & Repair
IPC RAM isolation · PIX suspension redesign · 50 kg reduction
▼

Two major field repairs. (1) AV compute IPC was rebooting continuously during field test — isolated a faulty RAM slot on-site, removed defective module, restored full system stability with zero lost time. (2) PIX autonomous platform had persistent rear-wheel drift — designed and integrated a custom tie-rod and preloaded suspension system from scratch, correcting toe-in/toe-out and reducing platform weight by ~50 kg.
